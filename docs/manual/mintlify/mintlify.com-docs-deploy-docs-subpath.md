English

Host your documentation at a subpath like `your-domain.com/docs` to keep your docs on your main domain, which makes them easier to find and maintains a cohesive brand experience for your users.

##

[​](#set-up-by-hosting-provider)

Set up by hosting provider

Setting up a `/docs` subpath varies depending on your hosting provider. Choose your hosting provider and follow the setup instructions.

- [Cloudflare](https://www.mintlify.com/docs/deploy/cloudflare): Set up Cloudflare Workers for your domain to serve your documentation at the `/docs` subpath.
- [AWS Route 53 and CloudFront](https://www.mintlify.com/docs/deploy/route53-cloudfront): Deploy your documentation at the `/docs` subpath using AWS with Route 53 DNS and CloudFront CDN.
- [Vercel](https://www.mintlify.com/docs/deploy/vercel#host-from-docs) - Use rewrites to deploy your documentation at the `/docs` subpath.
- [Custom reverse proxy](https://www.mintlify.com/docs/deploy/reverse-proxy) - For other hosting platforms, set up a reverse proxy to deploy your documentation at the `/docs` subpath.

###

[​](#additional-configuration-for-strict-security-policies)

Additional configuration for strict security policies

If you proxy all traffic on your custom domain, you may need to configure your proxy or firewall rules to add the correct [headers](https://www.mintlify.com/docs/deploy/csp-configuration) so your documentation displays properly.
